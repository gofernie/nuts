The Cedars

The Cedars is a peaceful residential neighbourhood in Fernie, BC, located just minutes from downtown and the ski hill. Surrounded by forest and trails, it offers a quiet, family-friendly setting with easy access to outdoor recreation. Homes are modern yet blend with the mountain environment. Residents enjoy biking, hiking, and skiing right from their doorstep, making it ideal for those seeking a relaxed, nature-oriented lifestyle year-round.

Riverside Fernie offers exceptional riverfront living along the pristine Elk River, combining stunning mountain views with quiet residential charm. This sought-after Fernie neighborhood features single-family homes and townhouses perfect for those seeking peace and natural beauty, making it one of the most desirable real estate locations in the Elk Valley.

Prime Riverside Location

Living in Riverside means direct access to the Elk River, creating a fisherman's dream location just steps from your door. World-class fly fishing opportunities await residents, while the flowing river provides a peaceful soundtrack to daily life. Spectacular mountain vistas frame this picturesque community, offering year-round natural beauty.

Outdoor Recreation Paradise

Riverside residents enjoy immediate access to Fernie's extensive trail network. The West Fernie Riverside Dyke Trail provides a gravel path along the Elk River, perfect for walking, cycling, and jogging. This pathway connects to 15km of connected trails around Fernie, accommodating all outdoor enthusiasts and skill levels.

Convenient Ski Access

Winter recreation is unmatched, with Fernie Alpine Resort located just five minutes away. This proximity makes Riverside ideal for skiing and snowboarding families who want mountain access while maintaining tranquil riverside living.

Close to Amenities

Despite its peaceful setting, Riverside maintains excellent connectivity. Fernie's city centre is just 3km away, providing easy access to shopping, restaurants, schools, and essential services without sacrificing the neighborhood's serene atmosphere.

Riverside Fernie represents premium mountain lifestyle living, where natural beauty meets modern convenience. Whether you're seeking adventure on the trails, relaxation by the river, or world-class skiing, this exceptional neighborhood delivers the complete Fernie experience with stunning real estate opportunities in British Columbia's most beautiful mountain setting.
