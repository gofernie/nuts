Other Areas

The Elk Valley and East Kootenay region of southeastern British Columbia offers unparalleled mountain wilderness experiences. Home to pristine lakes, towering Rocky Mountain peaks, abundant wildlife, and world-class recreation opportunities, this area provides authentic Canadian mountain living. Coal mining heritage meets outdoor adventure in communities surrounded by vast wilderness and stunning natural beauty.

Sparwood

Nestled in southeastern British Columbia's Elk Valley, Sparwood serves as the gateway to outdoor adventure. This coal mining community boasts the world's largest truck, stunning Rocky Mountain views, and year-round recreation opportunities. From hiking and fishing to snowmobiling and skiing, Sparwood offers authentic mountain living with modern amenities and warm hospitality.

Tie and Rosen Lake

These pristine alpine lakes near Fernie offer exceptional fishing and wilderness experiences. Tie Lake and Rosen Lake feature crystal-clear waters teeming with cutthroat and rainbow trout. Accessible by hiking trails, these remote destinations provide peaceful solitude, stunning mountain reflections, and unforgettable outdoor adventures for anglers, photographers, and nature enthusiasts seeking authentic wilderness experiences.

Elkford

Known as Canada's highest incorporated community, Elkford sits at 4,300 feet elevation in the Canadian Rockies. This mountain town offers world-class fishing, hunting, and hiking opportunities. Surrounded by pristine wilderness and abundant wildlife, Elkford provides an authentic Rocky Mountain experience with friendly locals, clean mountain air, and breathtaking scenery throughout all seasons.

Jaffray

Jaffray is a picturesque mountain village in the Kootenay region, offering tranquil lakeside living and outdoor recreation. Surrounded by pristine wilderness, this charming community provides excellent fishing, boating, and hiking opportunities. With its friendly atmosphere, stunning natural beauty, and proximity to larger centers, Jaffray represents the perfect balance of rural serenity and convenience.
